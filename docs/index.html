🚀 How It Works

1

Launch the Editor

Double-click run.vbs — no console, no install. The editor opens and waits for the game.

2

Start Far Far West

The editor auto-detects the game process and connects. You'll see 🟢 Connected within seconds.

3

Edit Your Values

Type an amount and click Set, or tap a preset. Gold and Souls update in real-time.

4

Freeze & Play

Click ❄ to freeze a value. Spend freely — the editor keeps it locked at your chosen amount.
